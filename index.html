The Lost Echo Studio

The Sound of Refinement

Preene Dias, a Trinity Grade 8 certified distinction holder with over 20 years of experience as a performer in India, is a highly skilled musician based in Bangalore. Throughout her career, she has been an integral part of numerous bands, choirs, celebrity collaborations, and acappella groups. Now, as a dedicated music teacher, she shares her wealth of knowledge with over 40+ students,guiding them to not only learn but enjoy music in the comfort of their own homes. Preene’s unique approach blends fun with learning, ensuring her students develop both their skills and their love for music.

Explore a world of genres! Pop, jazz, gospel, rock, a cappella, and beyond. Master the style you love while discovering new musical horizons.
